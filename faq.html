Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before you switch to electric. Can't find your answer? Contact us directly.

Charging & Range

How long does it take to fully charge a SYSEV scooter?

Charging time depends on the model: 3.5 hours for the Urban, 4 hours for the Swift, and 4.5 hours for the Swift+, all from 0–100% using the standard charger that comes with your scooter. You can charge from any home wall socket — no special outlet needed.

Can I charge the battery without taking the whole scooter inside?

Yes. All three models use a removable battery pack, so you can carry just the battery up to your apartment or office desk and charge it there, rather than needing to park the scooter near an outlet.

Does cold weather or monsoon rain affect range or charging?

Like all lithium-ion batteries, very cold temperatures can reduce range slightly, though this is rarely significant in the Kathmandu Valley's climate. All SYSEV batteries and connectors are sealed against monsoon rain and splashing.

Warranty & Service

What does the warranty actually cover?

Every SYSEV scooter comes with a battery warranty (3 years across all models, with mileage caps that vary by model) plus a separate vehicle warranty covering the motor, frame, and electronics. Full terms are listed on each model's spec sheet on the Scooters page.

Where can I get my scooter serviced?

Our Putalisadak and Pulchowk showrooms both have full service centers on-site. Suryabinayak and Boudha locations currently handle sales only — check the Showrooms page for current service availability near you.

Is the first service free?

Yes — every new SYSEV scooter includes free service for the first year, covering routine checks, brake adjustment, and software updates. Parts replacement outside of warranty is charged separately.

Buying & Financing

Do you offer EMI or financing options?

Yes, our showroom financing desks work with partner banks to offer EMI plans. Bring your citizenship document and income proof to a showroom visit, and our team will walk you through eligible plans on the spot.

Can I trade in my old petrol scooter?

We're piloting a trade-in program at our Pulchowk showroom. Bring your scooter's bluebook for an on-the-spot valuation — availability at other locations is expanding through the year.

How long after ordering will I receive my scooter?

In-stock models at a showroom can be ridden home the same day after paperwork. For models out of stock at your nearest showroom, typical delivery is 1–3 weeks depending on color and configuration.

Test Rides & Dealership

What do I need to bring for a test ride?

Just a valid driving license. If you don't have one yet, let us know when booking — our staff can still walk you through the scooter in a no-ride demo.

I want to become a SYSEV dealer in my city. How do I apply?

Head to the Contact Us page and select "Become a dealer" as your topic — our partnerships team reviews every application and will follow up to discuss requirements and territory availability.

When will SYSEV bikes and cars be available?

We're targeting 2027 for electric bikes and 2028 for electric cars. Visit the Coming Soon page to see our roadmap and sign up to be notified the moment reservations open.

Still have questions?

Our team is happy to help over phone, email, or in person at any showroom.
