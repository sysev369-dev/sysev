Range and charging

The M5 is listed with a claimed range of 330-350 km per charge under ideal conditions. Actual range varies with load, speed, riding style, terrain, weather and battery condition.
The supplier invoice lists three FX configurations: 72V-38Ah Graphene, 72V-30Ah LFP, and 72V-50Ah LFP, each supplied with its charger. The brochure lists a 90-100 km claimed range for the smaller options and 150-200 km for the larger LFP option under ideal conditions.
Charging time depends on the selected battery capacity, charger, starting charge level, and conditions. Ask the SYS EV team for the current charging specification of the exact configuration you are considering.
Battery installation and service access can vary by configuration. Confirm the charging and battery-handling process for your selected M5 or FX before purchase.
Yes. Range can change with rider and passenger weight, gradients, speed, stop-and-go traffic, tyre pressure, temperature, weather, and battery condition. Published ranges are stated for ideal conditions.
