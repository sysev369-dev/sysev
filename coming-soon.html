The road ahead

Scooters were the start. Two more vehicles are on the way.

We're building SYSEV step by step — proving ourselves on two wheels before we expand the range. Here's exactly what's coming and when.

Phase 1 · Live now

Electric Scooters

Urban, Swift, and Swift+ — three scooters available at our showrooms across the Kathmandu Valley today.

Phase 2 · Targeting 2027

Electric Bikes

Higher-powered two-wheelers built for highway commutes — think Kathmandu to Bhaktapur, or Pokhara's longer stretches. Bigger battery packs, faster charging, and a frame built for two riders.

Phase 3 · Targeting 2028

Electric Cars

A compact electric car sized and priced for Nepali families — easy to park in narrow lanes, with enough range for valley-to-valley trips.

2027

SYSEV electric bike, codename "Highway"

SYSEV electric car, codename "Valley"
