Our story

Built for the way Nepal actually rides.

SYS EV brings premium electric mobility to Nepal with useful range, confident power, connected features, and a growing local ownership experience.

How it started

A simple question that became a company.

Why should Nepali riders have to choose between petrol costs and electric scooters that do not match their everyday needs? SYS EV began by looking closely at the actual ride: steep roads, dense traffic, changing weather, passenger loads, and the need for dependable range.

That focus now takes shape in two models. FX gives city riders flexible battery and warranty choices. M5 extends the idea into a flagship long-range scooter with the same 2500W liquid-cooled motor platform.

Our mission

Make electric the obvious everyday choice.

Offer practical range, transparent configurations, modern rider features, and a local team that makes the switch easier to understand.

Our vision

Build a trusted electric mobility brand for Nepal.

Begin with scooters, learn from real owners, expand support city by city, and develop a broader electric line-up with care.

What guides us

Four commitments behind every ride.

Relevant performance

Range, motor power, suspension and braking selected for demanding daily roads.

Clear choices

Battery, range, warranty and price shown side by side so riders can compare properly.

Cleaner mobility

Electric scooters that replace everyday tailpipe journeys with quiet, efficient travel.

Local relationships

A growing showroom, service and dealer network built one dependable partnership at a time.
