Our story

Built in Nepal, for the way Nepal actually rides.

SYSEV Pvt. Ltd. was founded on a simple frustration: every scooter on our roads was designed for somewhere else's traffic, somewhere else's slopes, somewhere else's fuel prices.

How it started

A garage in Balkhu, a question we couldn't drop

In 2024, three engineers who'd grown up riding pulsars and scooties through Kathmandu's traffic started asking why no one had built an EV specifically for Nepali roads — the steep climbs to Swayambhu, the monsoon potholes of Ring Road, the daily stop-and-go of Putalisadak.

Two years of prototyping, testing on real valley roads, and conversations with hundreds of daily riders later, SYSEV Pvt. Ltd. was incorporated — and our first scooter rolled off the line.

Our mission

Make electric the obvious choice for every Nepali commuter.

Not because it's trendy — because it should cost less, climb better, and need less maintenance than the petrol scooter sitting in your shed right now.

Our vision

A full electric fleet, designed and assembled in Nepal.

Scooters today. Bikes and cars next. Eventually, a SYSEV showroom and service center within reach of every major town in the country.
